## What this session established (generator-free, no Claude window burned)

1. **Multi-session is NOT a packing/positioning problem.** Multi-session questions need a
   median of 3 gold sessions combined. The 1st gold session is at rank 1–2 for BOTH correct
   and incorrect items (so `evidence-first-pack` has no headroom — REFUTED). The separator is
   gold-session *coverage* in the contextTopK=8 window: CORRECT 54% full / INCORRECT 29% full.
   The 2nd/3rd gold sessions are stranded at ranks 16–190, under **distinct** distractor
   sessions (not duplicate chunks — session packing recovers 0/28). Only blunt context-depth
   reaches them, and only partway (8→20: +4 coverage; 8→30: +7; ~13 items dead at rank 33–190).
2. **Confound:** gold-in-window is necessary-but-not-sufficient. 46% of correct multi-session
   items won WITHOUT full coverage; P(correct | full coverage) ≈ 44%. So multi-session is
   fundamentally a **synthesis-from-deep-recall** problem; retrieval levers give +1–3 items max.
3. **Session packing (≤1 chunk/session) is a clean lever for PREFERENCE, not multi-session.**
   Preference gold sits at AvgRank ~41 because chatty sessions pile chunks above it. Packing@8
   pulls it into the window: **+14 preference + 3 temporal to full coverage, zero slot-1 loss,
   zero multi-session loss.** Flip headroom (currently-wrong subset): 6 preference + 1 temporal.
