## Critical Issues (Both Validators Agree)

### 1. ZERO RISK REDUCTION (13/14 sites)

**Gordon**: "No sample or guarantee = asking for Michelin-level trust with zero proof"

**CISO**: "Only Enterprise has a free sample. The other 13 ask for $495 sight-unseen from an unknown brand. That's a non-starter for first-time buyers."

**Impact**: PRIMARY conversion blocker across entire fleet

**Solution**: Add free sample download OR 100% money-back guarantee to ALL variants

---

### 2. ENTERPRISE HAS NO BUY BUTTON

**Gordon**: "Best trust-building in the fleet but can't close the sale - most frustrating variant to review"

**CISO**: "Does everything right on trust (free sample, bias tagging, Gartner takedown) and everything wrong on conversion (no pricing page, no buy button)"

**Impact**: Best marketing page that cannot sell

**Solution**: Add "Buy Full Report - $495" button next to every "Download Free Sample" CTA

---

### 3. FLETCHER WRONG BUSINESS MODEL

**Gordon**: "Selling consulting when delivering reports - fundamental mismatch"

**CISO**: "Not a product page. No pricing, no buy button, no reports. Wrong business model entirely. 3/12 score."

**Impact**: Damages brand consistency, confuses visitors

**Solution**: Rebuild as product sales page OR delete from lineup

---

### 4. WEAK CTAS ACROSS BOARD

**Gordon**: "Too many passive CTAs - 'Browse Reports,' 'Explore Services,' 'Get Started.' These are suggestions, not calls to action. Say 'BUY THIS REPORT - $495' and mean it."

**CISO**: "Buy button exists but flow unclear beyond click - no visible checkout"

**Impact**: Conversion friction at moment of purchase

**Solution**:
- Standardize strong CTAs: "Buy Report - $495"
- Complete Stripe checkout flow (button → payment)
- Make CTAs visually dominant

---

### 5. NO SOCIAL PROOF (ALL SITES)

**Gordon**: Did not assess (focus on presentation)

**CISO**: "Zero testimonials, zero customer counts, zero reviews. For a $495 product from an unknown brand, this is a significant barrier."

**Impact**: Trust gap for first-time buyers

**Solution**: Add testimonials, customer count ("Trusted by 50+ security teams"), or logo wall

---

### 6. MONITOR CREDENTIAL INCONSISTENCY

**Gordon**: Did not catch

**CISO**: "Monitor says '20 years' while all other sites say '30 years' - destroys credibility if buyer visits multiple variants"

**Impact**: Trust destruction on cross-variant comparison

**Solution**: Change Monitor to "30 years" everywhere
