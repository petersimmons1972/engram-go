User has direct enterprise cybersecurity remediation experience at scale. After the Home Depot breach (~2014), their firm received $5.5M in business to help remediate. This gives them firsthand knowledge of how "minimal change / accept risk" security postures lead to catastrophic breaches — specifically the pattern of flat networks + supply chain trust + deferred structural fixes.

This informs their strong stance against CISO-style "patch only critical/high" approaches. They know from experience that the compounding MEDIUM issues (network segmentation, RBAC, supply chain integrity) are where real breaches live.
