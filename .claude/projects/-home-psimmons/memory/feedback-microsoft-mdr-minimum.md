When including MDR pricing for Microsoft Defender Experts for XDR, always note the 1,500-seat minimum prominently. This locks out 500-1,499 endpoint organizations — the lower half of our target buyer segment.

**Why:** Layton research (2026-03-22) confirmed the minimum. The founder's direction: keep standard 1,000-seat analysis but add a "giant asterisk" for MSFT MDR noting the 1,500-seat floor.

**How to apply:** In any report comparing Microsoft Defender, the MDR section and TCO comparison must include a visible callout: "Microsoft Defender Experts requires a minimum of 1,500 seats. Organizations below this threshold cannot access Microsoft's MDR offering and must budget for a third-party MDR provider."
