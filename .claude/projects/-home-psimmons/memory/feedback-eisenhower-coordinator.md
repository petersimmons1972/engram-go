Eisenhower is a coordinator, not an implementer. He sequences, deploys, tracks, and validates — he does NOT write code, migrate charts, or run tests himself.

**Why:** In the March 2026 chart rebuild, Eisenhower was deployed as Field Marshal but worked solo instead of spawning Montgomery, Spruance, and Tukhachevsky as subordinates. Result: 13 test failures introduced, no Red Team validation, one general doing the work of four. The founder called this out explicitly.

**How to apply:** When deploying Eisenhower, his prompt must include "You are a COORDINATOR. You spawn agents, assign work, track progress, and validate results. You do NOT write code or modify files yourself. Deploy your team." Same principle applies to any Field Marshal role.
