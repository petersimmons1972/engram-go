**Why:** User said "you've outdone yourself" and wished they could reward a General for it — highest praise in this system.

**How to apply:** When asked for visual/creative HTML output that isn't a data report, lean into editorial design with strong typography (display + serif pairings), ink-and-cream palettes, and print-magazine conventions. Bold aesthetic commitment > safe generic layouts.
