# Premium AI Hardware Retail Distribution

**Context:** Researched ASUS Ascent GX10 (GB10 superchip, ~$3,500) availability on 2026-05-07.

## Key Finding
Premium AI supercomputers (>$3,000) have **highly constrained retail distribution**. Only specialist retailers carry them:

### Online Retailers (Confirmed in-stock sources)
- **ExcaliburPC** (excaliberpc.com) — explicit in-stock confirmation, expedited shipping available
- **ASUS eShop** (shop.asus.com/us) — direct from manufacturer, free shipping, BUT: all sales final / no returns policy
- **Central Computer** (centralcomputer.com) — San Jose-based, highly rated (4.8★), 2-day air shipping to Atlanta
- **Micro Center** — online + in-store pickup (see below)

### Local Atlanta
- **Micro Center** only retailer carrying GB10-class hardware locally
  - Marietta: 1275 Powers Ferry Rd SE, Suite 50
  - Duluth: 2340 Pleasant Hill Road
  - Stock is location-specific and constrained
- Best Buy, Walmart, Newegg: do NOT carry this class of product in-store or show reliable fulfillment

### What Doesn't Work
- General consumer retailers (Best Buy, Walmart) don't stock $3,500+ AI supercomputers
- Amazon can list it but fulfillment is unreliable for this SKU
- B&H Photo: out of stock, 7-14 day restock cycle

## Pattern
When sourcing premium/enthusiast hardware ($2,500+):
1. Check Micro Center if local pickup needed
2. Check specialist retailers first (ExcaliburPC, manufacturer direct)
3. Central Computer for price + rating + shipping combination
4. Avoid general consumer channels for this price/spec range

**How to apply:** For future premium hardware purchases, skip Best Buy/Walmart and go direct to specialist retailers or manufacturer eShop.
