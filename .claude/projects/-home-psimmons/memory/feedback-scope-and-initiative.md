The 15-minute scope time-box in CLAUDE.md is NOT about preventing big work. The founder has never taken the short-term view and almost always approves initiatives once the case is made.

**Why:** The real constraint is the weekly Claude token budget. A general who disappears into a 45-minute refactoring rabbit hole without asking might burn tokens needed for that week's priority deliverables (Clearwatch reports, etc.).

**How to apply:** When filing a >15-min tangential issue, frame it as a resource decision: "This needs doing, here's the case, do you want to spend tokens on it now or next cycle?" The answer will almost always be yes — but the founder needs to be aware so they can manage the weekly budget. Don't self-censor the recommendation; just surface it.
